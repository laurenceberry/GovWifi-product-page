Support

Support for service teams

GOV.UK Verify is built and supported 24/7 by a full-time team at the Government Digital Service.

Support updates

If you have a problem, check for known issues on the GOV.UK Verify status page first. You can subscribe to get updates whenever GOV.UK Verify creates or updates an incident.

Support in office hours

Our office hours are from 8:00am to 18:00pm (GMT), Monday to Friday. Use the GOV.UK Verify service team support form to get help and give feedback. The support team aims to respond to questions by the next working day.

Out-of-hours support

We offer 24-hour online support for critical incidents affecting live production services. We have different response times depending on whether the problem you want to report is an emergency.

It’s only an emergency if:

  • a significant number of users can’t verify themselves to access your service
  • you have reasons to believe there’s been a security breach
  • your ‘matching service adapter’ has stopped working

We’ll reply within 20 minutes and give you hourly updates until the problem’s fixed. We’ll reply by the next working day for any other problems.
